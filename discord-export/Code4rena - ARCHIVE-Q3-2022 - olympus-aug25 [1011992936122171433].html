When this style of section headers is used with code folding, the text of the header (the most important part) disappears. I'm all for people being creative and I do believe aestethics of a contract are important - especially when we're out here building hyperstructures to last for eternity. But I don't think it's worth breaking any kind of functionality for aestethics when there are literally limitless alternative styles to choose from.
